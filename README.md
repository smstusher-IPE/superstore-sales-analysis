# Superstore Sales Analysis

## Project Description
This project analyzes sales, profit, and product performance of a Superstore dataset to identify growth opportunities, seasonal trends, and product focus areas. The analysis is done entirely in **SQL**.

**Dataset:** Superstore dataset (Kaggle)  
**Tools used:** SQL (SQLite / Online SQL)

---

## SQL Queries
1. Total Sales & Profit
2. Sales by Region
3. Profit by Category
4. Top 5 Sub-Categories by Sales
5. Monthly Sales Trend
6. (Optional) Profit per Region × Category

---

## Insights

**Total Sales & Profit**  
- The store generated $X in total sales with $Y total profit. Profit margin is approximately Z%.

**Sales by Region**  
- South region contributed the highest sales ($X), but West region had higher profit margin ($Y). Suggests South may have higher discounts or lower efficiency.

**Profit by Category**  
- Technology drives the most profit, followed by Furniture and Office Supplies. Furniture has high sales but lower profit margins.

**Top 5 Sub-Categories**  
- Chairs and Bookcases are top revenue generators. Chairs deliver higher profitability than Bookcases → recommend marketing focus on chairs.

**Monthly Sales Trend**  
- Sales peak in November–December, consistent with seasonal shopping trends. Lowest sales occur in January–February, indicating a post-holiday dip.
