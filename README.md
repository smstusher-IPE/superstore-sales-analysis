# Superstore Sales Analysis

## Project Description
This project analyzes sales, profit, and product performance of a Superstore dataset to identify growth opportunities, seasonal trends, and product focus areas. The analysis is done entirely in **SQL**.

**Dataset:** Superstore dataset (Kaggle)  
**Tools used:** SQL (SQLite / Online SQL)

---

## SQL Queries
1. Total Sales & Profit
2. Sales by Region
3. Profit by Category
4. Top 5 Sub-Categories by Sales
5. Monthly Sales Trend
6. (Optional) Profit per Region × Category

---

## Insights

**Total Sales & Profit**  
- The store generated $2296195 in total sales with $286241 total profit. Profit margin is approximately 	12.47%.

**Sales by Region**  
- West region contributed the highest sales ($725255), and also the highest profit margin 14.94. The lowest sales is found in the south region ($391721), but the lowest profit margin is on central zone (7.92).

**Profit by Category**  
- Technology generated the highest sales ($836,154) with a profit margin of 17.4%, Office Supplies had sales of $718,735 and a margin of 17.02%, while Furniture had high sales ($741,306) but a very low profit margin of 2.49%. Technology and Office Supplies are driving profitable sales, while Furniture sells well but yields minimal profit, indicating a need for pricing or cost optimization. 

**Top 5 Sub-Categories**  
- Hones and Chairs generate the highest sales and profit, followed by Storage and Binders, while Tables show negative profit despite decent sales, highlighting a potential pricing or cost issue.
